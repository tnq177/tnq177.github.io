Toan Q. Nguyen

I'm a 4th year PhD student at University of Notre Dame, focusing on (Neural) Machine Translation for low-resource languages. My advisor is David Chiang.

I used to work as a freelance developer for a company called Tripchum where I was mentored by Christ, the founder, and the rising-author/developer President Huy. Then I spent some time at the wonderful EastAgile.

Before working on Neural MT, I was also very interested in Computer Vision and Image Processing in general. I self studied a bit with Learning OpenCV and this amazing book. I implemented some simple algorithms. My favorite one so far is the little planet image transformation

In my free time, I love to read about Vietnam's modern history, binge movies/tv shows (big fan of friends, frasier, himym, that70sshow...), listen to music or read books & manga.
